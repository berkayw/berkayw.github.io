Description

What You Can Do in This Game:
- Fight monsters, get EXP, and level up your character.
- Explore an island and a dungeon.
- Gather items, and buy/sell them with a merchant NPC.
- Parry the attack and give massive damage!
- Fight a boss!
- etc.
